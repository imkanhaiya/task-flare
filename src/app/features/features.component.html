Features

Made with your productivity in mind, Our feature-packed todo app keeps you organized. Check out the functionalities below to make the most of your day.

  • Add Todo: Quickly add new tasks to your list.
  • View Todos: Automatically see all your tasks on the home page.
  • Todo Details: Click 'Get Details' for more info on each task.
  • Edit Todo: Modify your tasks as needed.
  • Delete Todo: Remove tasks that are no longer needed.
